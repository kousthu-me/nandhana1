Beyond Serving Food

Nourishing Communities

With close to 5,000 meals a day served during the first COVID-19 wave, the momentum was kept alive during the second wave as well. In 2021, meals were also sent out to reach COVID-19 positive patients across the city — keeping in mind the requirements of a patient with utmost hygiene and quality ingredients.

Going beyond just serving food, Nandhana Palace has also contributed towards building a school in Bengaluru — bearing all expenses of books and uniforms for every child studying there.

Our Journey

A Story of Passion

"Nandhana — Sanskrit for daughter — a name given with love, built with dedication."

Dr. R. Ravichandar, born and raised in Sholinghur near Chittoor, Andhra Pradesh, moved to Bangalore for a degree in Commerce while supporting his family. Inspired by the Tamil film Manathil Urudivendum, he channelled that spirit of determination into founding Nandhana Palace in 2004 — named as a tribute to his daughters.

2004
Nandhana Palace founded in Bangalore. First branch opened in Koramangala.
2004 – 2015
Expanded to 9 branches independently across Bengaluru.
2015
Daughters joined the family business, accelerating expansion.
2015 – 2023
Grew from 9 to 25+ Andhra-style restaurants across Bengaluru and Chennai.
Today
27 branches. Pioneer of Andhra cuisine in Bangalore, recognised across India.
